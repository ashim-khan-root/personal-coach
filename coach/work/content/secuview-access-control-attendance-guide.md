# Access Control & Time Attendance Systems Qatar: Complete Guide

In Qatar, businesses are moving away from manual attendance registers and traditional keys. **Access control systems** manage who enters your building, while **time attendance systems** track when employees clock in and out — two sides of the same security coin.

Whether you run an office, school, warehouse, or retail shop in Doha, this guide covers everything you need to choose the right system.

---

## What's the Difference?

| | Access Control | Time Attendance |
|---|---|---|
| **Purpose** | Who can enter a door or area | When employees arrive and leave |
| **Output** | Door opens or stays locked | Logs date/time for payroll |
| **Typical users** | All employees + visitors | Employees only |
| **Integration** | Can link to CCTV, alarms | Can link to payroll software |

Many modern systems combine both — a fingerprint scan unlocks the door AND records your attendance at the same time.

---

## Types of Access Control & Attendance Systems

### 1. Fingerprint Recognition

The most common option in Qatar. Fast, reliable, and affordable.

**Pros:**
- Each fingerprint is unique — no buddy punching
- Affordable (500-600 QAR per device)
- Works in most environments

**Cons:**
- Can struggle with dry or dirty fingers
- Not ideal for workers with rough hands (construction, factories)

**Best for:** Offices, schools, retail shops

### 2. Face Recognition

Touchless and hygienic — grew in popularity after COVID-19.

**Pros:**
- No physical contact required
- Fast — identifies in under 1 second
- Works even if the person is wearing a mask (advanced models)
- Prevents buddy punching completely

**Cons:**
- Slightly more expensive
- Can be affected by lighting conditions

**Best for:** Offices, hospitals, schools, warehouses

### 3. RFID Card / Key Fob

Traditional card-based access. Simple and durable.

**Pros:**
- Very cheap per user
- Simple to issue and revoke
- Durable and weatherproof

**Cons:**
- Cards can be lost, stolen, or shared
- No biometric verification — buddy punching possible

**Best for:** Warehouse gates, parking, low-security areas

### 4. PIN Code / Password

Simple keypad entry.

**Pros:**
- No hardware to carry
- Easy to change codes

**Cons:**
- Codes can be shared or observed
- Least secure option

**Best for:** Low-traffic doors, storage rooms, villas
