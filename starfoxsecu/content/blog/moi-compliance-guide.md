## What is MOI Compliance?

The Ministry of Interior (MOI) in Qatar sets strict standards for security system installation and operation across the country. Any organization operating in Qatar must ensure their security infrastructure meets MOI requirements.

## Why MOI Compliance Matters

1. **Legal Requirement** — Non-compliance can result in fines and legal action
2. **Insurance** — Many insurance policies require MOI-approved systems
3. **Safety** — MOI standards ensure your facility is properly protected
4. **Business Continuity** — Compliant systems are more reliable

## Key MOI Requirements

### CCTV Systems
- Cameras must meet minimum resolution standards
- Retention period of at least 30 days
- Coverage of all entry/exit points
- Integration with MOI monitoring systems where required

### Access Control
- Biometric or card-based systems for sensitive areas
- Audit trail of all entry/exit events
- Emergency override capabilities
