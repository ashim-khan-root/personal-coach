## Why Legal Registration is Required

When you apply for the WhatsApp Business API, Meta needs to verify that your brand is a legitimate, legally registered entity. This is a core security requirement to protect users and maintain the integrity of the WhatsApp platform.

To pass this verification, you will be asked to provide **official government-issued documents** that include:

- Your brand's **Legal Business Name**.

- The **Physical Business Address**.

## Accepted Document Types

Since you mentioned having a brand rather than a company, you will still need to provide one of the following legal documents issued by a government authority:

- **Business Registration or License Document**

- **Certificate or Articles of Incorporation**

- **Government-Issued Business Tax Document** (such as a Tax Certificate)

- **Business Bank Statement**

**Note:** Self-filed tax documents, utility bills (for name verification), or unofficial brand names that aren't legally registered are not accepted for this process.
