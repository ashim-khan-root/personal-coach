About Starfox Security System

Starfox was established in 2005 with a focused aim at becoming a leading low current systems integrator in Qatar and regional markets. We provide BMS, GRMS, Audio Video, Control, Automation, and Security Solutions.

Our valuable experience and professionalism set the company as a key reference leader with high customer satisfaction across military, financial, medical, shopping, residential, and commercial sectors.
