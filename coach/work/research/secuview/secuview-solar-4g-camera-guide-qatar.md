**What Is a Solar 4G Security Camera?**

A solar 4G security camera runs on renewable solar energy and connects through the 4G mobile network instead of WiFi. It needs no electrical wiring, no internet connection, and no monthly electricity cost. The built-in solar panel charges a rechargeable battery that keeps the camera running 24 hours a day, 7 days a week — even during Qatar's cloudy winter days and during power outages.

---

**Why Security Cameras in Qatar Need Solar + 4G — Not WiFi**

Most security cameras sold internationally assume you have reliable WiFi and easy access to power outlets. Qatar's properties present three problems these cameras can't solve:

- **Villas and compounds** have large outdoor areas far from the router. WiFi signal drops before you reach the gate.
- **Construction sites** have no WiFi and no power installed yet. Security is needed from day one.
- **Remote properties** like farms, warehouses, and labour camps have neither internet nor electrical infrastructure.

A solar 4G camera bypasses all three problems. It inserts a local SIM card — Ooredoo, Vodafone, or STC — and connects to the 4G network directly. Installation takes 10 minutes with no electrician and no technician.

Yes — and the data proves it. Qatar averages over 300 sunny days per year. A high-efficiency solar panel generates enough charge in 3-4 hours of direct sunlight to power a 4G camera for 24-48 hours continuously. The camera housing is IP66-rated, meaning it is completely protected against dust ingress and powerful water jets. It operates reliably in temperatures from -20°C to 60°C — well above and below anything Qatar's climate produces. The solar panel itself is tempered glass rated for extreme UV exposure and sand abrasion.
