Frequently Asked Questions

Which SIM cards work with this router?

Any standard 2FF or 4FF SIM from Ooredoo, Vodafone, or other Qatari carriers. Dual SIM model also available for automatic failover.

Is this router compatible with 5G?

It supports 4G LTE. For 5G, please check our 5G router range. 4G LTE in Qatar is more than sufficient for most home and office needs.

Can I use it without a SIM card?

Yes — it also works as a standard WiFi router via the WAN port if you have a fiber or DSL connection.

How many devices can connect?

Up to 32 devices simultaneously without performance drops.

How long does the battery last?

Approximately 3-4 hours depending on usage — enough to ride through most power outages in Qatar.

Do I need installation?

No. Insert your SIM, plug in the power, and connect. Takes 2 minutes.
