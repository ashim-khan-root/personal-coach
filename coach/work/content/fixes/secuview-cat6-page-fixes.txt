<h2>Why Choose Bare Copper Over CCA?</h2>
<p>Bare copper cable conducts electricity better than Copper-Clad Aluminum (CCA). This means stronger signal strength, less voltage drop for PoE devices, and longer lifespan. For PoE cameras and access points, bare copper is the safer choice.</p>
