## CCTV as a Service

An innovative way of managing surveillance where Starfox offers CCTV and other security solutions through a subscription-based model.

We install surveillance hardware and software in return for monthly subscription fees. This reduces CAPEX spend where upfront capital costs are freed up to be better utilized elsewhere in your organization.

### Benefits of CCTVaaS:
- No large upfront investment
- Predictable monthly costs
- Always-updated technology
- Professional monitoring included
- Maintenance and support covered
