## Objective

Drive direct sales for secuview.com across multiple channels beyond organic SEO. Prioritize highest-ROI channels for a Qatar-based WooCommerce store with WP admin access only.

## Approach

Layer 5 growth channels in order of effort-to-impact:

### Phase 1 — Google Business Profile (1-2 days)
- Claim/verify secuview.com GBP listing for Salwa Road showroom
- Add products (up to 100), posts (weekly), Q&A, reviews
- Add photos of showroom, products, installations
- GBP posts with special offers → direct calls/visits
- **Impact:** Local "security store doha" / "cctv shop near me" searches

### Phase 2 — Google Shopping + Merchant Center (2-3 days)
- Install WooCommerce Google Listings plugin (WP admin)
- Set up Merchant Center account
- Create product feed → free listings + paid Shopping ads
- Focus on Smart Home, Network, Audio categories (user's categories)
- **Impact:** Products appear with price in Google Search — high-intent buyers

### Phase 3 — WhatsApp Business Catalog (1 day)
- Set up WhatsApp Business account on user's phone
- Upload product catalog (Smart Locks, Routers, Switches, Doorbells)
- Share catalog link in website header/contact page
- "Click to WhatsApp" button on product pages
- **Impact:** Qatar's #1 communication channel — direct sales conversations

### Phase 4 — Facebook/Instagram Shop (2-3 days)
- Connect WooCommerce to Facebook (Meta for WooCommerce plugin)
- Set up Instagram Shop (requires Facebook Shop)
- Tag products in posts/stories
- Run retargeting ads to website visitors
- **Impact:** Visual products (locks, doorbells, cameras) sell well visually

### Phase 5 — Google Ads Search (ongoing)
- Start small (50-100 QAR/day budget)
- Target high-intent keywords: "buy smart lock qatar", "4g router doha", "poe switch price qatar"
- Link to optimized category/product pages
- Track with conversion tracking
- **Impact:** Immediate traffic while SEO builds

## Deliverables

1. GBP fully optimized with products, photos, posts
2. Google Shopping live with product feed
3. WhatsApp Business catalog active
4. Facebook/Instagram Shop connected
5. Google Ads campaign draft with keywords, ad copy, budget

## Success Criteria

- GBP visible in local pack for "security store doha"
- Products appearing in Google Shopping
- WhatsApp Business catalog accessible from website
- Instagram Shop set up with products tagged
- Google Ads draft ready for review (not launched until approval)

## Resources

- Profile: WP admin only (no FTP/SSH), no premium tools
- Budget: unknown for ads — propose small test budget
- Skills needed: seo-audit, content-strategy, analytics
