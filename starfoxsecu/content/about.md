## Who We Are

Starfox was established in 2005 with a focused aim at becoming a leading low current systems integrator. Operating in Qatar and regional markets, Starfox provides BMS, GRMS, Audio Video, Control, Automation, and Security Solutions.

We are masterfully creating an experience for our clients providing a custom-made design with perfectly skillful installation. Furthermore, we offer a variety of comprehensive maintenance programs, consultancy studies, and monitoring services tailored to meet our clients' requirements.
