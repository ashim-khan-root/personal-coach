how to resolve

The "Unsupported post request" error occurs because your WhatsApp Business account is currently restricted from sending messages. The object ID '1174702532388522' could not be found or accessed due to these account-level restrictions.

To resolve this, complete the following steps:

1. **Complete Business Verification:** Verify your business in the . This is a mandatory requirement for using the WhatsApp Business Platform.
    
2. **Configure Payment Method:** Add a valid payment method in  to cover messaging costs.
    
3. **Template Approval:** Submit and receive approval for at least one message template, as this is required to initiate conversations.
    

Once these requirements are met, the API will allow you to generate tokens and send messages. You can monitor your verification and payment status directly within your  dashboard.
